About Me

I have 10+ years of experience across software engineering, infrastructure, SRE, and platform engineering. That path gives me a practical understanding of the full delivery lifecycle: how systems are built, shipped, operated, and improved over time.

Today, I focus on platform engineering with an automation-first mindset. I design internal platforms, reusable workflows, and governance models that reduce friction for engineering teams and make delivery more consistent at scale.

The problems I usually help solve are familiar: too much manual platform work, inconsistent delivery workflows, weak self-service, scattered standards, and systems that become harder to operate as engineering grows. My goal is to turn that recurring pain into clearer paved roads, better developer experience, and platform capabilities teams can trust.
